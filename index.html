Solution
How SeeBuild Works

Three steps — from pointing your device to a cloud-signed report. No paperwork, no Excel.

1

Point Your Device

Computer Vision analyzes elements in the frame — walls, rebar, structures — and compares them against project documentation in real time. Works on any mobile device.

2

Get AR Guidance

Deviations are overlaid directly on screen: lines, zones, and labels showing the exact size of the error. A voice assistant tells you what to fix and where.

3

Record & Sync

An inspection report is generated automatically and synced to the cloud. Client, contractor, and supervisor all see the same data — in real time.
