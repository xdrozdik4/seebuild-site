Solution
How SeeBuild Works

Three steps — from pointing your device to a cloud-archived report. No paperwork, no delays.

1

Point Your Device

Computer Vision analyzes structural elements in real time — walls, rebar, geometry — and instantly compares them to project documentation. Works on any mobile device: smartphone, tablet, or smart glasses.

2

Get Instant AR Feedback

Deviations appear directly on screen as AR overlays: lines, zones, and labels showing the exact error size. A voice assistant tells the team what to correct — without stopping work.

3

Auto-Report & Sync

An inspection report is generated automatically and synced to the cloud. The full decision history is preserved — accessible to all stakeholders in real time.

SeeBuild AI
A Hybrid AI Built on 20+ Years of Construction Reality

An AI that thinks like an experienced builder — and gets smarter on every project

SeeBuild has its own AI engine, designed from the ground up with the engineering philosophy described in the book "SeeBuild: I See What I Build". The AI doesn't automate decisions — it automates observation, so human judgment stays exactly where it belongs.

From day one, the system carries a global knowledge base of construction standards. With every new project, it learns — expanding its ability to detect, contextualize, and predict deviations before they become problems.

🌍

Country-Aware Building Codes

Automatically applies local standards — Ukrainian DBN in Ukraine, IBC in the USA, Eurocodes in Europe. The right code, in the right place, at the right moment.

🧠

Learns From Every Project

Each completed site enriches the global knowledge base — reducing recurring mistakes across all future projects, for all users of the platform.

🤫

Silent Until It Matters

No signal means the process is within limits. When attention is needed, SeeBuild speaks briefly and precisely — no noise, no distraction.

⚖️

Decisions Stay Human

The AI shows risks and trajectories — but never replaces the engineer. Responsibility stays with the person. SeeBuild supports thinking, not substitutes it.
