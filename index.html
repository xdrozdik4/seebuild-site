Solution
How SeeBuild Works

Three steps — from pointing your device to a cloud-signed report. No paperwork, no Excel.

1

Point Your Device

Computer Vision analyzes structural elements in real time — walls, rebar, geometry — and instantly compares them against project documentation. Works on any mobile device: smartphone, tablet, or smart glasses.

2

Get AR Guidance

Deviations appear directly on screen: AR overlay lines, zones, and labels with the exact size of each error. A voice assistant guides the team on what to correct and where — without stopping work.

3

Record & Sync

An inspection report is generated automatically and synced to the cloud. Client, contractor, and supervisor all see the same data — in real time, with a full decision history preserved.

SeeBuild AI
A Hybrid AI That Learns With Every Project

Not just a tool — a partner that grows smarter on every site

SeeBuild has its own AI engine, built on the philosophy described in the book "SeeBuild: I See What I Build" — an engineering mindset, not just algorithms. The system doesn't automate decisions. It automates observation, so that human judgment remains where it belongs.

The AI continuously learns from new projects, expanding its knowledge base — while already carrying the full weight of global construction standards from day one.

🌍

Country-Aware Standards

Automatically applies local building codes — Ukrainian DBN in Ukraine, IBC in the USA, Eurocodes in Europe. The right standard, in the right place.

🧠

Learns From Every Project

Each completed site enriches the global knowledge base. Patterns, deviations, and decisions accumulate — reducing recurring mistakes across all future projects.

🤫

Silent Until It Matters

SeeBuild knows when to stay silent. No signal means the process is within limits. When attention is needed, the system speaks briefly and to the point.

⚖️

Decisions Stay Human

The AI shows risks and trajectories — but never replaces the engineer. Responsibility stays with the person. SeeBuild supports thinking, not substitutes it.
